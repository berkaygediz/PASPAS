# Privacy Policy

PASPAS is designed to protect your privacy. It operates entirely offline and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## Data Collection

There is no telemetry, tracking, or analytics built into PASPAS. The application does not collect your IP address, system hardware details, or usage statistics.

## Local Processing

All operations such as scanning temporary files, analyzing the registry, and uninstalling programs happen locally on your computer. PASPAS reads your file system and registry only to find the junk files and invalid entries you choose to clean.

## Administrator Privileges

PASPAS requires Administrator privileges to access protected system directories (like `C:\Windows`) and system registry keys. These privileges are used strictly for cleaning and tweaking purposes. No background services are installed, and the app does not monitor your system when it is closed.

## Open Source

PASPAS is open-source. You can review the entire source code on [GitHub](https://github.com/berkaygediz/PASPAS) to verify exactly how it handles your files and system settings.
